Chicken N' Rice

Description

A filling recipe that the whole family will enjoy! There's nothing like a good chicken and rice casserole recipe that fills the tummy and the soul. With vegetables included, it's a great, healthy option.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked and chopped skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Add all ingredients to a 9x13 pan
  3. Mix well
  4. Cook in oven at 350 degrees F for 45 min
  5. Remove from oven and let sit to cool for 5 min
  6. Serve and enjoy!
