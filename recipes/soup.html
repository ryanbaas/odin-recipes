Soup

Description

This pumpkin roasted soup recipe will be a fall favorite for family and friends! Easy to make and not much prep time, this will be your go to dish for Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broth
  • 1 pumpkin
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Blend sweet potatoes and pumpkin together
  2. Add blended ingredients into mixing bowl and mix in all other ingredients
  3. Stir until thoroughly mixed, then add all ingredients into medium sized pan
  4. Heat over stove until boiling
  5. Turn off heat, let sit to cool for 5 min, and serve. Enjoy!
