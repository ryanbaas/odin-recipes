Cake

Description

Nothing to top off a delicious meal than a heavenly dessert! Bake this tasty chocoloate cake in under an hour and impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cups milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 oz chocolate
  • 1 tbsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 min
  4. Serve with vanilla ice cream and enjoy!
